Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join a WhatsApp group from this directory?
Browse the active groups list above, find a group that matches your interest, and click the green "Join group" button. We instantly verify the link is still working and then open WhatsApp where you can confirm joining the community. No registration is needed.
Is this WhatsApp group directory really free to use?
Yes — 100% free, lifetime, with no hidden charges. You can browse, join and submit groups without paying anything. There is no premium tier, no signup wall, and no paid promotion. The platform is supported by simple hosting and will remain free forever.
How do I submit my own WhatsApp group?
Scroll to the "Submit Your WhatsApp Group" form near the top of the page. Paste your group invite link (or up to 40 invites at once mixed with any text), and click "Submit for review". Our admin team verifies each submission within 24 hours and then publishes it.
Are all WhatsApp groups in the directory verified?
Yes. Every link goes through automated validity checks at submission. After publishing, our cron service re-verifies every active group daily and automatically removes any that have been reset, revoked, or made inactive.
What types of WhatsApp groups can I find here?
We organise groups across 12+ categories including Study, Business, News, Technology, Entertainment, Sports, Jobs, Shopping, Travel, Gaming, Motivation and Regional language groups. The mix grows every day as more communities submit their links.
What if a group I joined is not working anymore?
Click the red "Not Working" button on the group card to report it. If the issue is admin-only posting or messages turned off, click "Messages Off". Our admin reviews reports daily and removes confirmed broken groups.
Can I submit multiple WhatsApp groups at once?
Yes. You can paste up to 40 WhatsApp invite links in the submission box at one time, mixed with any other text. Our parser auto-detects every valid invite, removes duplicates, and processes them in a single batch.
Do I need to sign up or share my number?
Absolutely not. The platform requires no registration, no email, and no phone number. You can browse, join and submit groups completely anonymously.
Is it safe to join WhatsApp groups from this site?
We verify that every link technically works, but we cannot guarantee the behaviour of the people inside any given group. Use common sense — never share OTPs, banking details, or personal info. Leave any group that feels off and report it to us.
How often is the directory updated?
New groups are added daily as users submit them. Existing groups are auto-verified every 24 hours by our cron job, so the listings stay fresh and broken links are removed automatically.
Can I leave a comment or review?
Yes — scroll to the Community Comments section near the bottom of the page. You can post a comment with your name and message. We moderate the section to keep it clean and helpful.
Who runs this website?
WhatsApp Group Hub is an independent free service maintained by a small team of community enthusiasts. Read more on our About page or get in touch via the Contact page.
