Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know about how the directory works, organised in one place. If your question isn't here, drop us a message via the Contact page.

Getting Started

How do I join a WhatsApp group from this directory?
Visit the homepage, scroll to the "Active groups" section, find a group that interests you, and click the green "Join group" button. We re-verify the invite link in real time and then open WhatsApp for you to confirm joining the group.
Do I need to register or sign up?
No. There is no account system. You can browse, join and submit groups anonymously, with zero personal information required.
Is the directory really free?
100% free, lifetime, no hidden charges, no premium tier. The platform is intentionally lightweight and the operating costs are kept minimal so it can stay free forever.

Submitting Groups

How do I submit my own WhatsApp group?
Scroll to the "Submit Your WhatsApp Group" form on the homepage. Paste your invite link (up to 40 links at once), then click "Submit for review". Our admin team reviews and publishes within 24 hours.
Can I submit multiple groups at once?
Yes — up to 40 WhatsApp invite links per submission. You can paste them mixed with any other text and our parser will auto-extract the valid links and remove duplicates.
Why was my group rejected?
Common reasons include: invite link was reset / revoked at the time of review, the group has admin-only posting (so members cannot speak), the group violates our Terms of Service (illegal, hateful, scammy, or harmful content), or the link was a duplicate of an already-listed group.
How long until my group is live?
Approval typically happens within 24 hours of submission. Many submissions are reviewed faster than that.
Can I edit or remove my submitted group later?
Yes — send the invite link to our team with a quick note (remove or update). We action removal requests promptly.

Verification & Safety

Are all listed groups verified?
Yes. We auto-verify every link at submission, and our cron service re-verifies every active group every 24 hours. Broken or revoked links are removed automatically.
Is it safe to join groups from this site?
We verify the technical validity of every link, but we cannot control what happens inside any third-party group. Use basic safety practices: never share OTPs, banking info, or personal IDs in any group; leave groups that feel suspicious; and use WhatsApp's block / report features when needed.
What if a group is broken or off-topic?
Click the red "Not Working" or yellow "Messages Off" button on the group card to file a quick report. Our admin reviews all reports daily.

Categories & Discovery

What categories of groups are available?
We currently host groups across 12+ categories including Study, Business, News, Technology, Entertainment, Sports, Jobs, Shopping & Deals, Travel, Gaming, Motivation, and Regional language groups. The mix grows daily.
How often is the directory updated?
Daily. New submissions are reviewed every day, and existing listings are auto-re-verified every 24 hours by background jobs.

Comments

Can I leave a comment?
Yes — scroll to the "Community Comments" section on the homepage. Add your name and message and post. We moderate the section to keep it clean and useful.

Technical & Privacy

Do you collect my personal data?
Almost none. We don't ask for accounts, emails, or phone numbers. Standard server logs (IP, user agent) are kept short-term for abuse prevention only. Read full details on the Privacy Policy.
Is this site affiliated with WhatsApp or Meta?
No. WhatsApp is a registered trademark of Meta Platforms, Inc. We are an independent community directory and have no formal relationship with Meta.
I have another question — how do I reach you?
Use the Contact page — we read every message and reply within a few business days.
