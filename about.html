About WhatsApp Group Hub

WhatsApp Group Hub is a free, community-driven directory built to help people discover and join active WhatsApp groups across a wide range of interests. The web is full of dead group lists — links that were copy-pasted years ago, revoked invitations, or admin-only groups where you cannot even speak. We started this project to fix that.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make it effortless to find a real, working, active WhatsApp group on any topic — and just as easy for community admins to grow their groups. The web has billions of people, but a lonely community admin still struggles to reach the right audience. We bridge that gap with a clean, fast, free directory.

How the Directory Works

1. Submission

Anyone can submit a WhatsApp group invite link via the form on the homepage. There is no signup, no payment, and no premium tier. You can paste up to 40 links at once, mixed with any other text — our parser will extract the valid invites automatically.

2. Verification

Every submitted link goes through automated validity checks at the time of submission. We confirm the link format is correct, the invite is reachable, and we extract basic metadata like the group name and icon where available.

3. Admin Review

A real human admin reviews each submission within 24 hours to make sure it follows our community guidelines. Spam, scams, harmful or rule-breaking groups are rejected. Approved groups go live in the public directory.

4. Daily Re-Verification

Once a group is published, our automated cron jobs re-verify every active link every 24 hours. If a group's invite is revoked, reset, or made inactive, it is automatically removed from the directory. This keeps the listings fresh and trustworthy.

5. Community Reporting

Visitors can report any group they find broken, off-topic, or rule-breaking by clicking the "Not Working" or "Messages Off" button on the group card. Reports are reviewed by our admin and acted on quickly. This combination of automation and community oversight is what keeps the directory genuinely useful.

Why It Is Free

We believe the value of a community directory comes from being open and accessible. Charging users would shrink the audience and exclude exactly the people who would benefit most. The hosting and verification costs are intentionally kept minimal so the service can stay free indefinitely. There is no premium tier, no paid promotion, and no plan to introduce one.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect personal data — no signup, no email, no phone number is required to use the site.
  • We do not sell or share data. We don't have any to sell.
  • We do not operate the WhatsApp groups themselves. We are a discovery directory only — once you join a group, you are interacting with that group's admin and members.
  • We are not affiliated with WhatsApp or Meta Platforms. WhatsApp is a registered trademark of Meta Platforms, Inc.

Trust & Safety

While we automate verification and review every submission, we cannot fully control what happens inside any third-party WhatsApp group once you join. We strongly recommend basic online safety practices: never share OTPs, banking details, or personal identification with strangers in any group; report or leave groups that feel suspicious; and use WhatsApp's built-in block and report tools when needed.

If you encounter a group on this directory that violates our guidelines, please use the report buttons on the group card or get in touch via the Contact page.
