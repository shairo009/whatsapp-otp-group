Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains, in plain language, what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. What We Collect

We deliberately collect as little as possible. To use the directory you do not need to create an account, give us your email, your phone number, or any other personal identifier. The only data we store is what is technically necessary to operate the service:

  • Submitted WhatsApp invite links — when you submit a group via the form, we store the invite URL, the auto-fetched group name and icon (if available), and timestamps for review and re-verification.
  • Comments — if you post a comment, we store the display name you choose and the comment text along with a timestamp. Don't enter personal information you don't want public.
  • Reports — when you report a group as Not Working, Messages Off, or other issues, we store the report reason and any optional details you provide.
  • Standard server logs — like every web service, our hosting provider records basic request logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp) for short-term abuse prevention. These logs are not used to build profiles and are rotated automatically.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require any signup or account.
  • We do not collect your email, phone, name, or location.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers or behavioural advertising cookies.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent any data — we have nothing to sell.

3. How Submission Data Is Used

The WhatsApp invite links you submit are used solely to populate the public directory. The group name and icon (when WhatsApp makes them publicly available) are displayed on the group card. Re-verification cron jobs re-check these links every 24 hours to remove broken or revoked invites.

4. Comments and Public Content

Anything you post in the comments section is publicly visible. Please don't share private information you wouldn't want others to read. We reserve the right to remove comments that are spam, abusive, harmful, or violate our Terms of Service.

5. Cookies

We do not use marketing or tracking cookies. The site may use minimal first-party storage strictly for technical reasons (e.g. remembering UI preferences within a single session). No cross-site tracking is performed.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Vercel, which receives standard server-level information needed to deliver requests. When you click a "Join group" button, you are redirected to WhatsApp itself — at that point you are interacting with WhatsApp / Meta Platforms and their privacy policy applies.

7. Children

This service is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Data Retention

Submitted group links and comments are retained for as long as they are useful for the directory. Removed groups (e.g. revoked invites) are deleted from public listings; minimal anti-abuse records may be kept briefly to prevent re-submission of disallowed content.

9. Your Rights

You can request removal of any group link you submitted, or any comment you posted, at any time. Contact us via the Contact page with the relevant details and we will action the request promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Significant changes will be highlighted on the homepage.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach out via the Contact page.
